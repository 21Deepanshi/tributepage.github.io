Ashoka

Known as Ashoka the Great

  1. 304 BCE: Possible birthdate of Ashoka the Great.
  2. 268 BCE - 232 BCE: Reign of Ashoka the Great, third ruler of the Mauryan Empire.
  3. 268 BCE: Ashoka becomes emperor of the Mauryan Empire in India.
  4. 260 BCE: Ashoka conquers Kalinga. The bloodshed makes him remorseful, and he turns towards non-violence.
  5. 232 BCE: Indian ruler Ashoka dies and the Mauryan Empire declines within fifty years.
"No society can prosper if it aims at making things easier-instead it should aim at making people stronger."- Ashoka The Great
